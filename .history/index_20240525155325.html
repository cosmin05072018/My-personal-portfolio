About Me

I am Cosmin Morari and I want to master the skills of a real programmer.

Today marks a year since the world of programming fascinated me, and writing code has become a passion for me. A year ago, I started researching what IT means, and I watched a lot of videos on YouTube about it. Curiosity helped me get exactly where I needed to go. Because I did not know what I would like to do in a career as a future programmer, I decided to read about every programming language and came to the conclusion that the Front-End suits me. At one point I realized that alone and without a mentor would not go too far, and that I would probably get bored of my failed attempts at learning. That's why I decided to enroll in some programming courses, and a friend recommended me to enroll in Wantsome - The Friendly IT Academy. Since I was new to the world of programming, I took the first step by starting an introductory course in programming in which I flirted with Python. The second step followed immediately after completing the introductory course in programming. I took the Front-End course which I graduated at the end of 2021, where I gained knowledge of html, css, javascript and a bit of React and typeScript.
At the moment I am looking forward to an internship, in which to develop the knowledge I have gained so far.
