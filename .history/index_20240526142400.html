About Me

I am Cosmin Morari and I want to master the skills of a real programmer.

The world of programming fascinated me, and writing code became a passion for me. A few years ago, I started researching IT and watched many YouTube videos on the subject. Curiosity helped me get exactly where I needed to be. Because I didn't know what I would like to do in a career as a future programmer, I decided to read about each programming language and came to the conclusion that Front-End was the right fit for me. At some point, I realized that alone and without a mentor I wouldn't get very far and that I would probably get bored with my failed attempts at learning. That's why I decided to sign up for a few coding courses, and a friend recommended I sign up for Wantsome - The Friendly IT Academy. Being new to the world of programming, I took the first step by starting an introductory programming course where I flirted with Python. The second step followed immediately after completing the introductory programming course. I took the Front-End course, which I graduated at the end of 2021, where I gained knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and a little React and TypeScript. Afterwards, more precisely a few months ago, I followed a training where I studied Backend where I studied PHP and Laravel in detail, but also where I developed many skills (communication, attention to details, research).
At the moment I am looking forward to an internship, in which to develop the knowledge I have gained so far.
